About Skoll

Taking care of houseplants while balancing a busy lifestyle can be challenging. Skoll is here to change that. Our smart plant pot **monitors your plant's environment** and **moves itself towards the best light source**—so your plant gets the care it needs, even when you're away.

Equipped with **sensors that track soil moisture, light levels, humidity, and temperature**, Skoll adjusts in real time to create the perfect growing conditions. To keep it stable, the pot moves along a **designated track** using a built-in line tracking system.

Want to see how your plant is doing? Our **web dashboard** provides live data, allowing you to monitor conditions and track trends through easy-to-read charts.
