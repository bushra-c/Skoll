About Skoll

In contemporary society, individuals are often away from home during daylight hours, making plant care a challenge. Skoll provides an innovative solution by integrating **real-time environmental monitoring** with **automated movement** to ensure houseplants receive optimal sunlight and care.

Our smart plant pot continuously **monitors key environmental parameters** such as **soil moisture, light intensity, air humidity, and temperature**. When necessary, the pot **moves along a predefined path**, guided by a **line tracking module**, to adjust to the best available light.

To provide users with **real-time insights**, we have developed a **web-based dashboard** that visualizes all recorded data. This allows users to track plant health and environmental conditions effortlessly.
